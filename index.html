Slime Peril

You are a slime. One that has traveled the lands far and wide. Until one day, as you're looking on into the distance, you find yourself taking one wrong step. A mere second later, you're falling down a hole. To your shock, you find yourself not just in any cave, but the layer of a dragon. Sadly he's not too keen on visitors.

Dodge the dragons' fiery attacks as you try to make your escape.
